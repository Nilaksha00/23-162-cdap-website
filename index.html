Advancing Healthcare through Eye Diseases Detection and Classification

Project Details

Welcome to our groundbreaking research on Diabetic Retinopathy (DR) and Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD). These two eye conditions are significant contributors to visual impairment and blindness worldwide. Our research focuses on developing advanced diagnostic tools using cutting-edge image processing and machine learning techniques, with a particular emphasis on their applicability in resource-limited regions around the world.

We are dedicated to creating a mobile application utilizing deep learning models that can accurately detect and categorize the severity of DR through the analysis of retinal fundus images. Our goal is to facilitate early detection, enhance treatment effectiveness, and improve the overall diagnostic process. With our mobile app, you can take control of your eye health. Simply upload your retinal images, and let our cutting-edge technology do the rest. Receive early detection and detailed annotations, all in the palm of your hand. Our app is designed to streamline the diagnostic process, making it faster, more accurate, and more accessible.

Join us in the mission to reduce the global burden of blindness and visual impairment. With this mobile application, we're making a significant impact on eye health, one retinal image at a time.
